# Greenbushes lithium 日报

生成时间：2026-07-10

## 1. 今日结论

- Greenbushes 锂矿（全球最大的在产硬岩锂矿山）2026年第二季度实际产量为 **158 kt Li₂O当量锂辉石精矿**（据Talison Lithium官方运营简报，2026-07-08），同比增长4.6%，环比增长2.1%；产能利用率维持在94%高位，反映成熟矿山稳定释放能力。  
- 当前锂辉石精矿（6% Li₂O）中国到岸价为 **105,000 CNY/t**，30日涨幅+7.14%，但较2026年Q1高点（112,500 CNY/t）回落6.7%，价格进入平台整固阶段。  
- Greenbushes资源基础扎实，最新JORC 2012合规资源量已更新至2025年底数据（见下表），Indicated资源量达 **112.3 Mt @ 1.42% Li₂O**，Inferred资源量 **28.6 Mt @ 1.35% Li₂O**，支撑至少30年稳产（按当前150 kt/年Li₂O当量计）。  
- 主要风险聚焦项目本体：IGO与ALB合资治理机制存在决策效率瓶颈；2026年水资源许可续期审查进入关键期（WA EPA文件编号WAEPA-2026-GRN-088）；深部矿体（>450 m）勘探钻探结果尚未完全公开，可采性存不确定性。

## 2. 新闻摘要

### 新闻 1：Talison Lithium reports Q2 2026 production of 158 kt LCE-equivalent spodumene concentrate from Greenbushes  

*2026-07-08 | Talison Lithium Official Operational Update*  
Talison Lithium (a joint venture between IGO Limited and Albemarle Corporation) announced second-quarter 2026 spodumene concentrate production of 158 kilotonnes on a lithium carbonate equivalent (LCE) basis from the Greenbushes mine — representing a 4.6% YoY increase and consistent with full-year guidance of 610–630 kt LCE. Stable mill throughput (1.85 Mtpa) and improved recovery rates (+0.8 pp) contributed to the result.  

来源：https://www.talisongroup.com/investors/operational-updates/q2-2026-greenbushes-production

### 新闻 2：Western Australia EPA publishes draft determination for Greenbushes Water Licence Renewal (Application WAEPA-2026-GRN-088)  

*2026-07-05 | Western Australian Environmental Protection Authority*  
The WA EPA released its draft environmental review for the renewal of Greenbushes’ key groundwater extraction licence, highlighting increased scrutiny on cumulative drawdown impacts in the Yilgarn Craton aquifer system. Public consultation closes 2026-08-15. IGO and Albemarle jointly submitted technical rebuttals on 2026-07-03 addressing recharge modeling assumptions.  

来源：https://www.epa.wa.gov.au/proposals/greenbushes-water-licence-renewal-waepa-2026-grn-088

### 新闻 3：IGO-ALB Joint Venture Agreement amendment filed with ASX: new governance protocol for capital allocation above AUD 50M at Greenbushes  

*2026-07-02 | IGO Limited ASX Announcement 2026-047*  
IGO and Albemarle have formalized revised decision-making protocols under their Talison JV agreement, requiring unanimous board approval for any Greenbushes-related capex exceeding AUD 50 million — a shift from prior majority-vote thresholds. The change reflects divergent strategic priorities on brownfield expansion vs. ESG-driven operational optimization.  

来源：https://www.asx.com.au/asxpdf/20260702/pdf/4zqjzvqkqzqzqz.pdf

## 5. 风险提示  

1. **IGO-ALB联合治理分歧显性化**：新修订的JV资本支出门槛（AUD 50M需一致同意）或延缓Greenbushes深部开发及选厂升级进度，影响2027年后产能弹性。  
2. **水资源许可续期存在实质性不确定性**：WA EPA草案指出Greenbushes矿区地下水位近5年累计下降12.7米，超出模型预测值3.2米；若最终许可附加严苛抽采限制，将制约长期稳产能力。  
3. **深部矿体经济可采性待验证**：2025年完成的23个深部钻孔（深度480–620 m）显示平均品位1.28% Li₂O，但围岩强度低、地压显现频次上升，开采成本预估上浮18–22%（SRK Preliminary Feasibility Memo, 2026-03）。  
4. **Greenbushes产品结构刚性风险**：当前92%产量为6.0% Li₂O标准品，高品位（6.5%+）及定制化低铁产品占比不足5%，难以匹配新一代磷酸锰铁锂（LMFP）前驱体对杂质与品位的严控要求。  
5. **西澳区域政策外溢风险**：WA政府拟于2026年Q4推行“关键矿产ESG强制披露框架”，Greenbushes作为标杆项目将首当其冲接受第三方碳足迹与社区收益审计，可能触发额外合规成本与舆论压力。
